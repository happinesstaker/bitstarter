With this new campus robot,either transporting things or moving around the whole campus area becomes incredibly easy!This special robot features 50kg lifting ability, GPS/Visual positioning technique, also user friendly operating.All these will make your campus life easier.

The functioning of the robot needs the computer made map of the whole campus,which can be mapped by our auto-mapping robot or given by you.No special road or facility is needed to support this robot,except some charging places.Robots can moving in almost all kinds of landscape in campus accurately and rapidly, with nearly no probability to contact with walker-bys.Kind of excellent robot!
